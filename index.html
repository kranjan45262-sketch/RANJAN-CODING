Our Story

About Chanchala Golden Crust

Chanchala Golden Crust, Chatra (Jharkhand) ki ek premium bakery hai, jahan har product ghar jaisi mehnat aur pyaar se banaya jaata hai. Hum maante hain ki achha khana sirf swadisht nahi, balki hygienic aur healthy bhi hona chahiye.

Bread se lekar cakes tak, cookies se lekar rusk tak — hamari har cheez fresh ingredients se, saaf-suthre tareeke se aur bharose ke saath taiyar ki jaati hai.

Humara maqsad hai aapke ghar tak asli, taaza aur pyaar se bana khaana pahunchana.
